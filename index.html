    About PCs and Parts Africa

    We are committed to changing lives through access to computing. Our quest to democratize the entire computing ecosystem has broadened in scope, where we are providing a marketplace for computer shops to buy, sell, and trade their computer parts with independent buyers and other shops.
    Our main objective is to develop a full-functioning computing ecosystem that allows for the local assembly of affordable computers and widespread access to parts. Specifically, we aim to build the ecosystem for the making of affordable, reliable, locally assembled computers.
