PCs & Parts Africa Group

We are committed to changing lives through building horizontals and opening up spaces for access to computing in Africa

Our Mission

Our team at PCP Africa Group has launched a series of initiatives with African Universities and local communities to make computing more accessible in Africa. We firmly believe that building the foundation for a modular computing ecosystem that relies on computer parts already accessible in Africa and educating those who desire access to computing to circularly build and maintain using these parts is the first step to a democratized and sustainable computer space.

Impact Programmes


The core focus of PCP Africa Group is to leverage global and regional technology networks to accelerate the democratization of computers in Africa through an open-access for all.

Other priority areas include:

  • Grassroot Impact & Learning
  • Emerging Technologies and Innovation
  • Futures Leadership and Governance
  • Education for All
  • Entrepreneurship & Equity

Community 


The PCP Africa Group community is growing already! As we partner with new organizations, universities and local communities we are increasingly finding new and exciting ways to get inspired builders and learners in Africa involved in developing our impact programs. Ultimately, PCP Africa Group is mobilising resources (human, technological and financial) for the African people, and scaling up these programmes with a plan to pass on the torch to those that truly understand the African untapped potential and opportunities.
