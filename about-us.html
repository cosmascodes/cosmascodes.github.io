About Us

The PCs and Parts (PCP) Africa Group is a non-profit, socially-responsible, and technology-oriented organization that works towards making computing in Africa accessible for all school-going pupils and university students. The Group is the charity arm of the PCs and Parts Africa Limited - an e-commerce platform committed to easing the sourcing, trading, and circularity of computers and parts in Africa.
