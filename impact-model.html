Our Impact and Sustainability Model

How do we want to avert this and ensure access to computing for all?

Up until recent, governments and big technology companies have been placing a lot of fodder on building verticals and creating tech hegemonies around the world. This, for a very long time, has led to deep and/or created new inequalities and barriers around access to computing and informatics. The implication is the locking of millions of young people out of infinite opportunities that have existed since the advent of computers and if these walls persist, African Universities and by extension, grassroot communities, will again be left out of the future digital space & economy.
